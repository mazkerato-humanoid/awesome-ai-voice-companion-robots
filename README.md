# Awesome AI Voice Companion Robots Under 1000€ (EU Focus) 🚀

> **The ultimate curated collection of low-cost home robots (humanoid & non-humanoid) with natural voice interaction** — ready to talk like Grok, Gemini Live or local LLMs.  
> All entries **≤ 1000 €**, available or shippable in Europe, with strong emphasis on **data privacy** and **EU AI Act compliance**.

**Why this list?**  
In 2026 the European market still lacks affordable voice-AI companions that respect privacy and EU regulations. This living repository tracks commercial products **and** open-source/DIY projects you can turn into full Grok/Gemini-like voice companions using Ollama, Whisper, Piper TTS and xAI/Gemini APIs.

**Focus areas**  
- Price cap: **≤ 1000 €** (all prices in €, VAT included where possible)  
- Voice-first: natural conversation (not just commands)  
- EU-ready: shipping, warranty, privacy notes  
- Local-first preference (Ollama / self-hosted)
